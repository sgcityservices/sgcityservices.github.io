False Alarm at Pizza Pizza

Published: February 17, 2025

Author: Vill Ager

The City Fire Services responded to a call at a local business shortly after 4 PM on Thursday, February 20. Two trucks responded, and firefighters were seen on the roof doing checks. Shortly thereafter, everything was determined okay.

Authorities have confirmed that there was no real threat, and the alarm was triggered by a mechanical issue. Shoppers and employees were briefly evacuated but allowed to return shortly afterward.
